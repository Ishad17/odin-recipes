Pizza




Homemade pizza begins with a simple dough of flour, water, yeast, and olive oil.
Spread a flavorful homemade tomato sauce, top with your favorite cheeses, and
customize with fresh veggies or savory meats. Bake until the crust is golden
and the cheese melts, yielding a delicious and personalized pizza straight from your kitchen.
Enjoy the creative freedom and satisfaction of crafting your own culinary masterpiece.



Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chees
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    2. - Mix flour, water, and yeast to form a pizza dough. Knead until smooth and let it rise until it doubles in size.
  2. Make Tomato Sauce:
    3. - Blend or cook tomatoes with garlic, salt, and pepper to make a simple tomato sauce.
  3. Preheat the Oven:
    4. - Preheat your oven to a high temperature, typically around 475°F (245°C).
  4. Roll Out the Dough:
    5. - Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    6. - Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
    - Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese (mozzarella) over the sauce.
    - Add sliced bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes on top.
  6. Bake:
    7. - Carefully transfer the pizza onto a preheated pizza stone or a baking sheet.
    - Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Finish with Olive Oil:
    8. - Once out of the oven, drizzle the pizza with a bit of olive oil for added flavor.
  8. Slice and Serve:
    9. - Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing. Enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!
