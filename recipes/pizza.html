Pizza

Homemade pizza begins with a simple dough of flour, water, yeast, and olive oil.
Spread a flavorful homemade tomato sauce, top with your favorite cheeses, and
customize with fresh veggies or savory meats. Bake until the crust is golden
and the cheese melts, yielding a delicious and personalized pizza straight from your kitchen.
Enjoy the creative freedom and satisfaction of crafting your own culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chees
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
